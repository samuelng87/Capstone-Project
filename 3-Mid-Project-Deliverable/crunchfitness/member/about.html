Meet Crunch! It’ll blow your mind.

We have a No Judgments philosophy here. It's our Monday-thru-every-day mantra. A simple philosophy that drives us to create a community and a gym for all. No judgments means room for everyone, regardless of shape, size, age, race, gender or fitness level. No matter your workout of choice, we want you to feel good while reaching your goals.

Oh yeah, it’s that good. See for yourself.

Our gyms have an easy vibe to them, they're massive, designed to offer you more variety and space to keep you motivated. We set low membership prices with no lock-in contracts to suit all budgets so there's no barriers to making fitness a lifestyle.

Our Teams. Checkmate.

And lastly. Meet our local owners!

So these two have combined, over 45 years’ experience managing in the fitness industry (yeah, they don’t look that old do they!?). It’s hard to find a couple as passionate and positive for people as these two, they call it ‘living with mana’, and it shows in everything they do. From the way they lead their Crunch family, with heart and intention, to the way they fearlessly grow the business, we are proud to call them our leaders. In 2007 they opened their first club in the heart of Sydney, partnering with some other young/not so young well respected fitness pioneers globally. It’s been a humble climb, however their unrivalled belief in ‘where there’s a will there’s a way’ has resulted in a steady opening of new clubs across the states of NSW and VICTORIA over the years. And the dream to grow across Australia is still in tack, we seem to always have far more vision than we do resources so we’re always grateful for the fun ride these two take us on. You’ll hear them say this everyday “everyone is created for greatness” and they show us just how true this is each time we witness a person share how Crunch changed their life .
